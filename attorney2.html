任茉茉律师是Sun & Ren PLLC的成立合伙人之一。任律师专精雇佣类和亲属类移民和非移民签证申请。任律师所代理的大，中，小型公司客户遍布各行各业，如金融，地产，技术，工程，娱乐，时尚，餐饮和教育行业等。至今任律师已成功代理上百件H-1B, L-1, O-1, TN, 劳工纸，EB-2/3, EB-5和亲属移民的申请。此外，借助其丰富的商业知识，任律师也成功帮助协约国投资者在美国通过创业取得E-1和E-2签证，以及帮助才华杰出者获得人才类绿卡和工作签证。任律师经常在企业和高校中做关于移民法，规则和政策的演讲，也为企业做关于I-9，E-Verify和H-1B Labor Condition Application的合规咨询。

在成立Sun & Ren PLLC之前，任律师在纽约知名华人律所担任高级移民律师多年，成功代理多家上市公司和国企客户的雇佣移民申请。任律师也曾在华盛顿特区司法部长公共利益办公室实习，期间接触到许多重要的民事诉讼。 任律师全奖毕业于乔治华盛顿大学法学硕士项目，并获得对外经济贸易大学的法律学士学位，持有纽约律师执照。任律师精通中文和英文，是美国移民律师协会的会员，并在2018年获得“Super Lawyer 年度新星：商业移民”的荣誉。

Momo Ren, the co-founder and partner of the firm, practices exclusively in the fields of employment-based and family-based immigration law. With a focus on analyzing and providing guidance on nonimmigrant and immigrant visa strategies to meet each client's specific needs, she has successfully assisted clients in obtaining H-1B, L-1, O-1, TN, PERM, EB-2/3, EB-5 as well as family-based immigration filings. Prior to founding Sun &Ren PLLC, Ms. Ren gained significant experience with reputable US-China law firms representing public and state-owned companies, as well as start-up, small, and medium-sized organizations on a wide variety of employment-based immigration matters. She has experience servicing a large volume of clients across diverse industries, including finance, real estate, technology, engineering, entertainment and fashion, hospitality as well as schools. She holds an LLM degree from the George Washington University, and an LLB from the University of International Business and Economics.

Ms. Ren also has extensive experience advising on complex matters, including working with entrepreneurs to obtain E-1 and E-2 visas for start-up enterprises and alien with extraordinary ability in certain fields. She regularly counsels clients regarding I-9 compliance, E-Verify and H-1B Labor Condition Application (LCA) compliance.

Ms. Ren speaks Mandarin and English. She is a frequent lecturer of immigration law, regulation, policy and compliance in front of private organizations and educational institutions. She is a longstanding member of the American Immigration Lawyers' Association.
