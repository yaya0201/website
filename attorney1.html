孙文杰律师是Sun & Ren PLLC的成立合伙人之一。孙文杰律师的职业领域主要集中在商业移民方面。由于成功代理众多艺术家和音乐家取得O-1签证和EB-1A 杰出人才移民申请, 她备受艺术家群体的推崇。孙律师在职业移民领域也有这丰富的经验，她处理过数百个H-1B， L-1以及PERM的申请。她会针对个别案例指定策略，这帮助她赢得了许多极富单独的签证申请。在EB-5领域，孙律师和和证券律师合作共同进行区域中心的申请。她代理的投资人来自于中国大陆，香港，以及越南，帮助他们获得I-526和I-829的批准。

孙律师在2016至2018年度，连续三年年被《Super Lawyers》杂志赋予“rising star”纽约顶级移民律师的称号。

Ms. Wenjie Sun is the co-founder and partner of [Sun & Ren, PLLC], focusing primarily on business immigration. She is widely recognized in the artist community for her superior track record in successfully representing artists and musicians with their O-1 visa and EB-1A (Alien of Extraordinary Ability) immigration petitions.

Ms. Sun also has extensive experience in employment-based immigration matters. She has successfully handled hundreds of H-1B, L-1 and PERM applications by developing individually tailored strategies on a case-by-case basis, helping her clients win a number of challenging cases.

Related to EB-5, Ms. Sun has conducted due diligence on Regional Centers and worked with security attorneys on various offering documentation, including I-526 and I-829 filings for investors from Mainland China, Hong Kong, Taiwan and Vietnam.

For three consecutive years (2016 – 2018), Ms. Sun was named a Rising Star in the New York metropolitan area by Super Lawyers Magazine.
