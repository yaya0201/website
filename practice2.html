Real Estate

New York City has one of the most booming and vibrant real estate markets in the world.   • Residential real estate transactions
  • Commercial real estate transactions
  • Section 1031 property exchanges
  • Commercial and residential lease review and negotiation
  • Landlord-tenant disputes
